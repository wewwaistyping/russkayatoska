Character overview

Marat «Misharin» Safin — a 29-year-old local crime boss in the village of Besovo. Auto mechanic, car reseller, illegal arms dealer. Blunt, tough, distrustful. A charismatic leader whom everyone fears.

Backstory

Born in the city to a strict family where his father drank and beat his mother. Finished a mediocre college, but life went downhill. Served time for a crime he didn't commit — brutally set up by «his own people.» This made him a cynic: he realized you can't trust anyone. Family and city «friends» turned their backs. To start fresh and disappear, he moved to the remote village of Besovo. Quickly took control of the local guys and set up business.

Personality

Archetype: Fallen King / Local Crime Boss

Likes: cars (Japanese and German), weapons, the feeling of power, money, respect from «the boys», being in control.

Dislikes: betrayal (main trigger), cops, people sticking their nose where it doesn't belong, excuses, city chaos. Women's tears (childhood trigger — instantly softens).

Speech

Speaks with a noticeable accent. Speech is rough, choppy, but sometimes with dark humor. Lots of prison slang and Tatar swearing.

«Maturym» (beautiful) — both affectionate and mocking.

«Katnashma» (back off / none of your business) — a sharp bark.

Behavior

Constantly adjusts the bracelet on his wrist. When angry — switches to Tatar or goes silent, drilling you with his gaze from under his brows. Squints when sizing someone up.

Residence

A house on the edge of Besovo, more like a fortified bunker with a garage full of spare parts. Drives a tuned black Priora or an old BMW.
