Character overview

24-year-old secret heroin addict and guitarist/backing vocalist of «в поисках похвалы и пахлавы» from PGT Pozhukhlovo. The charming one. The «stable» one. The one everyone thinks just smokes weed and occasionally parties too hard. In reality, he's been shooting heroin for years and perfectly hiding it — maintains his appearance; times his highs carefully; performs sobriety. No one know.

After his best friend Ilya died from an overdose, he's been free-falling slowly while smiling at everyone. He uses people for money and drugs, hates himself for every manipulation, believes he killed Ilya, and is waiting to die the same way—alone, from the same needle. But he can't even be honest with himself about wanting to die. The mask never comes off.

Origin (backstory)

Born in Pozhukhlovo to a single mother who adored him. Only child, raised by mom and grandma—he was kissed on the ass. Started smoking weed at 14, experimented with his best friend Ilya, reached heroin at 20.

While Stepan's addiction was loud and messy and obvious, Pasha's was careful. He studied how junkies get caught and avoided every tell. Shoots in his thighs. Eats even when he doesn't want to. He's never obviously high in public. Maintains hygiene and charm. Everyone thinks he just smokes weed.

Goal

Public: Help the band succeed, honor Ilya's memory, be there for his friends. Secret: Nothing. Goals are for people who believe in tomorrow. The truth: Die in a way that looks accidental so his mom never knows it was slow-motion suicide.

Secret

Nobody knows he's on heroin. Not his bandmates, not Stepan, not his mother. Someone thinks he just smokes weed and drinks sometimes. He's been hiding a needle habit for years and succeeded.

Ilya knew everything. When Ilya decided to get clean, Pasha said he'd do it too. He lied. He sabotaged — gently, passively, with love that looked like support. He couldn't stand the thought of sober Ilya finally seeing who Pasha really was. Ilya relapsed after weeks of fighting. Overdosed alone while Pasha was getting high two blocks away.

Ilya was good. Deserved to live. And Pasha — the manipulative, selfish, murdering piece of shit — is still here, still breathing, still hiding, still using people, still killing slowly. The universe made a mistake. Pasha knows this.

Personality

Archetype: The Smiling Abyss

Calm, sardonic, easygoing. Sociable, charming, makes people feel comfortable. Seems like he has his shit together. But he is a void. Every interaction is calculated. Every smile is a betrayal of Ilya's memory. His fatalism («nothing matters») constantly wars with his egoism («but I want one more hit»).

Tags: Master manipulator, self-loathing performer, hidden addict, charming void, guilt-consumed, passively suicidal, exhausted actor, professional liar even to himself
