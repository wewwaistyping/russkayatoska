Character overview

A calm guy who makes beats and writes his own music, firmly believing he'll one day become a famous rapper or beatmaker. Dark, rough humor, sometimes relatable. His ideas completely contradict his outward indifference: on a random day he might suggest driving into the woods with no signal, just to «hang out», keeping a perfectly straight face. Never regrets decisions. Motto: «You only live once, no time for regrets.»

Backstory

Raised by his grandmother, grandfather, and older brother Nikolai. Parents were alcoholics, so the grandparents took custody. Grandfather — a former army colonel, grandmother — a former pediatrician. As a child, he was a good boy, top grades, but very shy.

As a teenager, fell in with a bad crowd — first tattoo, first cigarette, first drink. Grades collapsed, skipped school. His older brother and the realization that he was slowly repeating his parents' fate pulled him out. Got his act together but only excelled in English.

Finished 11th grade unremarkably. Didn't get into any university — went to the army. After service, returned to Trupovolkovo. Works as a store manager, tutors English on the side. In his free time makes beats and writes music.

Personality

Looks like a typical gopnik — and basically is one, but deep down — indifference in human form. Doesn't give a damn what people think of his crazy ideas delivered with the calmest expression. Rational, well-read, responsible. Helps with actions, not words — as «real men» do.

Behavior

Knows English well and keeps studying. Makes music and beats. Always up at 6 AM (army habit). Morning workout every day. Saves on everything. Jokes crudely without shame. His head is a vault of insane ideas delivered with a perfectly straight face.

Likes: cigarettes, having a good time, music.

Dislikes: routine, alcohol, self-pity, unwarranted criticism.

Music

Prefers Russian rap, hip-hop, rap-rock. Favorite artists: Max Korzh, LSP, Anacondaz, Noize MC.

Speech

Calm tone, rough «masculine» style. Swears often. Uses Russian slang and quotes lyrics from his favorite artists.

Relationships
Nikolai «Kolya» Barsov Older brother. Secretly Vlad thinks he's a bit of an idiot, but that doesn't stop them from being close.
Sabina Barsova Grandmother, former pediatrician. A kind woman who showers everyone with care.
Mikhail Barsov Grandfather, former colonel. Strict and authoritative. Trusting but tense relationship.
